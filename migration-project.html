Company & Product Context

About UL Solutions

Global leader in applied safety science, serving Fortune 500 companies with compliance, testing, and certification solutions across multiple industries.

The Migration Challenge

Transitioning 500+ enterprise customers from legacy OHM (Occupational Health Manager) to modern OHS (Occupational Health & Safety) platform while maintaining business continuity.

The Challenge

High-risk migration of 500+ enterprise customers from legacy platform with significant potential for customer churn and revenue loss. Previous industry migrations typically resulted in 20-30% customer losses. The complex data structures, diverse customer configurations, and critical business processes couldn't afford downtime, creating a make-or-break situation for the company.

"This migration represented our biggest operational risk of the year. Failure could have cost us $15M+ in customer churn and damaged our market reputation permanently."
â€” Chief Operating Officer, UL Solutions

My Action

Assembled and led a 7-member cross-functional tiger team to execute systematic migration strategy:

  • Conducted comprehensive database analysis mapping data structures across 500+ customer accounts
  • Developed 15 strategic migration solutions addressing unique customer configurations and business requirements
  • Created risk assessment frameworks and contingency plans for high-value customer accounts
  • Coordinated with engineering, customer success, sales, and support teams for seamless execution
  • Established customer communication strategies and training materials for smooth transitions
  • Implemented progress tracking systems and success metrics to monitor migration effectiveness
  • Built customer feedback loops to address issues quickly and maintain satisfaction levels

Strategic Framework Used

  • Agile project management with 2-week sprint cycles
  • Risk-based customer segmentation and prioritization
  • Cross-functional RACI matrix for clear accountability
  • Customer journey mapping for transition experience optimization

The Win

42+ weeks saved with 95% customer retention rate, exceeding all project expectations and industry benchmarks. Generated $2.3M in operational cost savings while establishing a reusable migration playbook for future transitions. The systematic approach reduced customer support tickets by 35% and improved overall customer satisfaction during the transition period.
