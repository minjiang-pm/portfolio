Company & Product Context

About UL Solutions

Global leader in applied safety science, serving Fortune 500 companies with compliance, testing, and certification solutions across multiple industries.

The Platform

Enterprise sustainability software helping organizations track environmental metrics, manage ESG compliance, and generate regulatory reports for stakeholders.

The Challenge

Our sustainability platform was losing ground to competitors with fragmented workflows and disconnected user experiences. Enterprise clients struggled with manual data entry across multiple systems, leading to compliance gaps and inefficient reporting. Customer satisfaction scores were declining, and we were losing key enterprise deals to more integrated solutions.

My Action

Led a comprehensive customer-first strategy to transform our product approach:

  • Conducted 58 in-depth interviews with Fortune 500 sustainability directors and EHS managers
  • Performed competitive analysis of 12 major sustainability software vendors
  • Developed customer journey maps and persona frameworks to guide product priorities
  • Collaborated with engineering, sales, and customer success teams to translate insights into roadmap changes
  • Established quarterly customer advisory board meetings with key enterprise clients
  • Created go-to-market strategy for new platform features and pricing models

The Win

15% revenue growth and significantly stronger market position through customer-driven product enhancements. Increased platform adoption by 32% among existing customers, reduced churn by 18%, and won 3 major enterprise deals against specialized competitors.
